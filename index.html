Goal for Good — One goal, one smile

The Goal for Good initiative donates €5 for every official goal scored by our partner club. Funds support education, health, and basic needs projects in African communities.

How it works: We record official goals, calculate the monthly donation, and transfer it to verified NGOs.
  • Who: Players, the club, and the fan community
  • What: €5 per goal scored in official competitions
  • Transparency: Monthly reports and receipts available for all
Contact: info@charityfootball.org  |  Site: charityfootball.org
